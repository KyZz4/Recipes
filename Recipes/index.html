Recipes for falcon sausage with pasta

    8 grilled sausages or wieners
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • A few drops of tabasco, sriracha or some creamy chili flakes to taste
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 0.5 krm black pepper
    •

Cooking

  1. Start by boiling water for the pasta. Start cooking the pasta according to the package.
  2. Peel and chop yellow onion and garlic (optional).
  3. Slice the sausage and fry in olive oil in a pan with high edges.
  4. Add onion and fry gently for a few minutes on a lower heat while the pasta cooks. Add chili flakes if you want it in.
  5. Drain the spaghetti.
  6. Mix the spaghetti with the sausage, onion and ketchup in the pan, the pasta pot or a large bowl.
  7. Spaghetti with sausage or bacon is ready to serve.
  8. Season with chili such as tabasco or sriracha sauce and salt and black pepper.
